# Why Term Insurance Matters

Term insurance protects your family financially if something happens to the earning member.

It is one of the most important foundations of financial planning.

---

## Why People Buy Term Insurance

- Family protection
- Income replacement
- Loan protection
- Child education security
- Long-term peace of mind

---

## Common Mistake

Many people delay buying term insurance until it becomes expensive.

Starting early usually means:

- Lower premium
- Better coverage
- Easier approval

---

## Important Note

Term insurance is protection — not investment.
